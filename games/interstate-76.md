## Notes  

- Designed for Win95/98; behaves correctly only under 98SE timing.  
- Software renderer is the most stable and accurate mode.  
- Gold Patch 1.06 required; Nitro Pack recommended for full content.  
- Glide/D3D flags work but are limited to 640×480 and may reduce stability.  
- VM execution strongly recommended; native Win10/11 installs require wrappers and manual registry setup.  

⚠️ Stable under 98SE in 86Box  
Avoid native execution unless following pasture‑toolshed wrappers.
