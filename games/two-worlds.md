## Notes

- Patch 1.7b required for disc installs.  
- Use Windows Vista compatibility mode + DPI override (Application).  
- Disable fullscreen optimizations.  
- Game ignores driver-level framerate caps — use RTSS for a strict 60 FPS limit.  
- Autosave and quicksave can cause crashes; disable autosave and avoid quicksave.  
- Text input fields may not accept typing — use copy/paste.  
- Missing DX9 components require the DirectX June 2010 Runtime.  
- Missing voices/music require K‑Lite (WMA9 codecs).  
- AMD GPUs may crash unless installed on the C: drive.

⚠️ Stable under Windows 10/11 with RTSS 60 FPS cap  
Avoid framerates above 60; physics and animation break.
